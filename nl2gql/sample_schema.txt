Entities and properties:
- Person: id, name, age, salary, title
- Company: id, name, industry
- City: id, name, state

Relationships:
- (Person)-[:WORKS_AT]->(Company)
- (Person)-[:LIVES_IN]->(City)
- (Company)-[:LOCATED_IN]->(City)

Notes:
- Salaries are numeric (annual).
- Cities have unique (name, state) pairs.
